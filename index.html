Lucas Counsel is a boutique combining top-tier law firm and in-house general counsel experience to provide corporate and transactional representation to company and venture capital clients.

What We Do

Our clients are venture-backed and self-funded private companies, entrepreneurs, and private equity funds.   • Investor-side deal representation

Who We Are

Kevin Lucas began his legal career at Gunderson Dettmer, a Silicon Valley law firm specializing in the representation of emerging companies and venture capital investment firms, where he advised startup companies regarding corporate governance, equity financings, mergers & acquisitions, and IPOs.

Subsequently, Kevin Lucas served as General Counsel of Art.com, Inc., an international e-commerce retailer, where he advised senior management and the board of directors and managed all of the company’s legal needs related to IP protection and licensing, commercial contracts, corporate acquisitions, employment agreements and incentive plans, privacy and consumer laws, and dispute resolution.

Prior to forming Equity Corporate Law, Kevin Lucas was a Principal of Agiletic Law Group.

Kevin Lucas received his law degree from the UC Berkeley School of Law in 1998, and a Bachelor of Arts degree in microbiology from UC Santa Barbara in 1991.
